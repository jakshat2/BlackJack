How To Play

    Rule

  • Beat the dealer’s hand to get 21 or as close to as possible
  • If you get 21 points exactly on the deal, that is called a “blackjack.” (Ace and a ten value card)
  • The dealer and you have two cards. You have all cards face up and the dealer has one card up and one card face down

    • Card Values

  • Cards 2-10 are worth the value of the number on the face of the card
  • Face cards (Jacks, Queens and Kings) are worth 10. Ace can be either 1 or 11

    • The Player's choice

  • HIT - you can get one more card
  • STAND - you don’t want any more cards

    • Who is winner?

  • After you called "Stand," the dealer turns their card. If dealer has 16 or under then they must take another card
  • Win: If you have a blackjack, or you get higher points than the dealer
  • Lose: If you have gone over 21, or the dealer has same or over points than you
  • Blackjack can only beats blackjack. So if the dealer and you both have blackjack, game is Draw. Otherwise the person who has blackjack win
